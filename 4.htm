Summaries

Mahi, a newly married woman, brings an antique Jewish box into her home. When Mahi and her husband Sam begin to have paranormal experiences, they soon learn that the box is a dybbuk containing an evil spirit. The couple then seeks the help of a rabbi to unravel its mystery. Will they survive this ordeal before their child is born? In Mauritius an antique store watchman gets killed after he tries to open a Dybbuk,while in India Sam (Samuel) Issac and his wife Mahi decide to locate to Mauritius for his new job. Sam gets employed at a nuclear plant at a higher post while Mahi is trying to overcome her miscarriage she visits the same antique shop and buys the Dybbuk following which she starts seeing a ghostly women in the house who asks for Ezra. Sam at first feels its her hallucination but a priest who visits them confirms that Mahi could be possessed as she opened the Dybbuk and asks him to meet Rabbi (Jewish Priest) Benjamin.Sam meets Benjamin who introduces him to his son Rabbi Markus and tells him how ancient Hebrews used Dybbuk to capture spirits. At the same time Sam gets call from Mahi confirming her pregnancy where Benjamin tells him that the evil spirit will try to capture body of their child for revenge.Upon reaching home Sam sees that Mahi has been possessed and the evil spirit has taken control of her body before things could go out of control Markus comes to their aid and decides to find details about Ezra.
